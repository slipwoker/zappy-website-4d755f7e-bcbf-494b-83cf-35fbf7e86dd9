    Our Advantages

    Precision. Cleanliness. Professionalism.

    GROUTBOT — a machine for filling grout in joints — is currently being developed for professionals who demand accurate and perfect results in every job. Three main advantages of working with GROUTBOT

    Ease and speed

    GROUTBOT is designed to enable uniform and precise filling of joints in any width and depth. The application head is adapted to the joint lines and ensures complete and uniform coverage — without exhausting work on your knees.
    Most importantly, the machine will advance at a fast rate of over 200 sqm in 8 working hours including machine operation time.

    Clean and organized finish

    The machine's advanced control mechanism is designed to reduce excess grout and prevent stains on tile surfaces. Significant reduction in cleaning time after work — the floor will remain clean, and you'll focus on the final result rather than cleaning up the mess.

    Suitable for harsh field conditions

    Designed to withstand demanding projects — renovations, new construction, commercial flooring and contracting work. The robust body and powerful motor ensure consistent performance throughout long working hours, on any type of surface and grout.
